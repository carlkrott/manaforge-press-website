Manaforge Press opens its doors today.

We are a fantasy and LitRPG publishing imprint built around a simple conviction: genre fiction readers deserve worlds that feel lived-in, magic systems that feel earned, and characters who carry the weight of real consequences.

Our catalogue launches with three authors, each occupying a distinct corner of the genre landscape.

**R.K. Ashvane** writes grimdark fantasy where the prose is measured and the stakes are absolute. Classic storytelling cadence meets the dark fantasy edge. If you appreciate John Gwynne's ability to blend Norse-inspired darkness with emotional stakes, or Anthony Ryan's rhythm-focused storytelling, Ashvane is your next read.

**D.F.Bruno** delivers high-energy progression fantasy where every fight scene crackles with urgency. LitRPG systems woven into compelling narrative, not pasted on top of it. Comparable to the pacing architecture of Will Wight's Cradle series or Django Wexler's military fantasy.

**V.K. Hollow** specialises in dungeon crawler adventures with emotion-based magic systems. Tactical combat treated as a puzzle. Characters whose inner state directly impacts their power. For readers who love Jeff Sproul's character-driven LitRPG or J.R. Castle's underground adventures.

Four books are complete. More are in the forge.

Sign up for the newsletter below to be notified when our first titles hit Amazon and Kindle Unlimited. We'll also be dropping free preview chapters, world maps, and character art in the coming weeks.

Welcome to the forge. Let's build something worth reading.
